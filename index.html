Hey! I'm a high school student who is driven to solve meaningful problems and play a role in nudging our planet toward a safe and sustainable future. The idea of letting this planet go down in flames has always frightened and infuriated me, so today, my passion is to eliminate the threat of climate change. With this ambition, I've spent the past several months taking part in a human accelerator program called The Knowledge Society, which has acted as an enlightening and door-opening creative playground. It was through this program that I realized my potential as a teenager, and with the help of powerful mindset training and exposure to exponential technologies, I've been diving deep into alternative energy, while discovering our current options for rapidly scalable and economically viable climate solutions.

As a constant learner who strongly believes that there's no way better to learn than to fail, I love to throw myself into new and uncomfortable challenges. In light of this, forming new connections and staying on top of my goals is always a priortiy for me, so if you would like to follow along on my journey of pursuing my ever-evolving definition of success, a subscription box to my newsletter and a contact form can be found below.
